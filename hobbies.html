More About My Hobbies

A hobby of mine that takes up a majority of my time is track and field. I run short distance as well as throwing javelin. While throwing javelin I have become a 2x National Qualifier as well as meeting some of my friends I have at college.

Another hobby of mine is traveling whether that is in state or out of the country. The picture above is from Jamaica this past summer. I enjoy spending time with my family and friends on these vacations and relaxing from school.

I also enjoy competing on the Columbia College's FBLA and DECA teams. Being apart of these teams have been an amazing opportunity to meet new people as well as grow my own ability in the business world.

Then finally I enjoy being outdoors. I grew up on a farm so being outside has a special place in my heart. Some activites I enjoy include bike riding, fishing, and golfing.
