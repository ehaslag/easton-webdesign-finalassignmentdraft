More About My Graphic Design

I enjoy spending time creating graphics using mainly Photoshop and Illustrator. The majority of my graphics come from working directly with the Columbia College track and field team. I create a wide varitey of posts such as meet days, school records, signees, national rankings, honors, and awards.

I also enjoy making sports graphics on the side as well. The majority of the graphics I create are for basketball players I enjoy watching or people my friends enjoy cheering for.
